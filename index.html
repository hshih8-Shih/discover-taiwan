Explore the beauty, flavor, and culture of Taiwan

Discover Taiwan is a beginner-friendly travel guide that introduces popular destinations, famous foods, and practical travel tips for visitors planning a memorable trip.

Why I chose this topic

I created this website to introduce Taiwan as a vibrant travel destination. Taiwan offers beautiful natural scenery, rich cultural traditions, exciting cities, and amazing street food.

What visitors can find here

This homepage gives an overview of the site and leads users to featured destinations, food ideas, and helpful travel resources.

Featured Destinations

Taipei

The capital city is known for Taipei 101, night markets, temples, and convenient public transportation.

Jiufen

A charming mountain town with lantern-lined streets, tea houses, and scenic coastal views.

Taroko

Famous for dramatic marble gorges, hiking trails, and breathtaking natural landscapes.

Taste of Taiwan

Bubble Tea

One of Taiwan's most famous drinks, loved for its chewy tapioca pearls and refreshing flavors.

Beef Noodle Soup

A classic comfort food that combines rich broth, tender beef, and handmade noodles.

Night Market Snacks

From fried chicken to scallion pancakes, Taiwan's night markets are full of affordable local favorites.

Quick Travel Resources

  • Best time to visit: spring and autumn
  • Easy payment options: cash card and transit card
  • Transportation: MRT, train, high speed rail, and bus
  • Popular experiences: night markets, temples, mountain views, and local cafés
