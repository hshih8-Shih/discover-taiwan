Taste of Taiwan

Bubble Tea

Bubble tea is one of Taiwan’s most famous drinks. It is enjoyed for its refreshing flavors, sweet milk tea, and chewy tapioca pearls.

Beef Noodle Soup

Beef noodle soup is a classic Taiwanese comfort food made with rich broth, tender beef, and noodles. It is one of the most iconic dishes in Taiwan.

Night Market Snacks

Taiwan’s night markets offer many delicious local foods, including fried chicken, scallion pancakes, grilled items, and sweet desserts. They are a must-visit part of the travel experience.
