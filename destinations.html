Featured Destinations

Taipei

Taipei is the capital city of Taiwan and one of the most convenient places for visitors. It is known for Taipei 101, night markets, temples, shopping districts, and efficient public transportation.

Jiufen

Jiufen is a mountain town filled with narrow streets, lanterns, tea houses, and beautiful coastal views. It is one of Taiwan’s most charming travel spots and is popular for its nostalgic atmosphere.

Alishan

Alishan is one of Taiwan’s most famous mountain destinations. It is known for its forest railway, sunrise views, giant cedar trees, and cool mountain air. Many visitors go there to enjoy scenic walking trails and the peaceful natural environment.
